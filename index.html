About Posters

Frequently Asked Questions

KAGADA is an Annual National Level Technical Student Conference conducted by IEEE UVCE. It will be held on 26th October, 2024.

KAGADA has 3 tracks: Paper Presentation, Poster Presentation, and Project Presentation. You can choose any one of these tracks.

As KAGADA is a national level technical student conference, it provides a platform to exhibit innovative ideas and showcase presentation skills.

There is no specific domain for presentation; the goal is to come up with ideas for the betterment of society.

KAGADA 2024 will take place at UVCE, KR Circle.

Yes, participants can take part in more than one track.
